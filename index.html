The Journey
FROM LESSONS TO LAUNCH
THE DAY JOB
Working as a Full Stack Developer — coding both server and client for companies. Building real production systems every day. But the dream was always to ship my own product.
THE 100M VND LESSON
A student once trusted me with publishing their game, Craft Runner. I gave it my best shot, investing in marketing and user acquisition — but in the end, I spent around 100 million VND without turning a profit. Still, it wasn't a loss in every sense. The experience taught me valuable lessons about ad strategy, user acquisition, and market timing — insights that will stay with me far longer than the money spent.
THE MARKETING LAB
Built a system: license games from SellMyApp, commission art from CraftPix, hire devs for content and apps, then handle SDK integration, monetization, and publishing myself. Each launch = another lesson in what works and what doesn't.
100K+ DOWNLOADS
Pets Hospital Simulation crossed 100K+ downloads on Google Play. Validation that the marketing and monetization knowledge was paying off.
LTAI.AI
Purchased a licensed AI SaaS source code, then customized, configured, and deployed LTAI.ai — a full AI platform powered by Claude Opus & GPT. 70+ templates, AI Chat, Images, Voiceover, Video, Code Gen. Plans from $9.99/month. Full-stack skills applied to real SaaS operations.
STEAM DEBUT
A student trusted me with their project — Dino Run Puzzle. I polished the game, fixed bugs, added content, and published it on Steam with a demo + DLC strategy. It didn't turn a profit either — but it marked a meaningful milestone: my first PC game on Steam. A door that once felt far away was now open.
NOW — THE REAL CHAPTER
An AI SaaS platform running. 11+ games & apps shipped. Marketing lessons absorbed. Full-stack skills sharpened daily at work. Now developing LTA Framework — my own game development framework, written from scratch. The tools are ready. The real games are coming.
