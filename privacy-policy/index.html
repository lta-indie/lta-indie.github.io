Luster To Alive - Privacy Policy

ABOUT LUSTER TO ALIVE

Hey there! I'm a solo indie developer behind LUSTER TO ALIVE. By day, I work a regular job — but nights and weekends? That's when the magic happens. I pour my heart into building games and apps that I genuinely love creating.

So far, I've released mobile games like Pets Hospital Simulation and Super Commando on both Android and iOS. Each project is a labor of love, built from scratch with care and lots of coffee.

I take your privacy seriously — probably more than big companies do. Your data is only collected when absolutely necessary, and I will never sell or share it with anyone. Period.

Want to know more about how your data is protected? Keep reading below.

VOICE CHAT AND MICROPHONE ACCESS

The Application includes a real-time voice chat feature that requires access to your device's microphone. Microphone access is only activated when you are actively participating in a voice chat session within the Application.

Voice data is transmitted in real-time directly between players in the same game session using Photon Voice, a third-party real-time communication service provided by Exit Games GmbH. We do not record, store, or retain your voice data on our servers. Once a session ends, the audio stream is discarded and cannot be retrieved.

You may revoke microphone permission at any time through your device's system settings. Please note that revoking microphone access will disable the voice chat functionality.

TEXT CHAT AND USER-GENERATED CONTENT

The Application includes a real-time text chat feature that allows players to communicate during gameplay. By using the chat feature, you agree not to send or transmit any content that is:

  • Offensive, abusive, harassing, or threatening
  • Sexually explicit or inappropriate
  • Discriminatory based on race, gender, religion, nationality, disability, or sexual orientation
  • Illegal, fraudulent, or in violation of any applicable law
  • Spam, advertising, or unsolicited promotional material

We reserve the right to monitor, moderate, and remove any content that violates these guidelines without prior notice. Repeated or severe violations may result in a temporary or permanent ban from chat features or from the Application entirely.

Text chat messages are transmitted in real-time and are not permanently stored on our servers. However, if a report is submitted against a user, limited chat logs may be temporarily retained solely for the purpose of reviewing and resolving the reported incident. These logs are deleted once the review is complete.

REPORTING AND BLOCKING

The Application provides tools for users to maintain a safe and respectful environment:

Report: You may report any player whose behavior or content violates our community guidelines directly within the Application. All reports are reviewed and appropriate action will be taken, which may include warnings, muting, or permanent account suspension.

Block: You may block any player to prevent them from communicating with you via voice or text chat. Blocked players will be unable to send you messages or join your voice sessions.

To submit a report outside of the Application, you may also contact us at: lta@lta-indie.com

THIRD-PARTY COMMUNICATION SERVICES — PHOTON ENGINE

Voice chat and text chat features in the Application are powered by Photon Engine, a real-time communication platform developed and operated by Exit Games GmbH, based in Hamburg, Germany.

When you use voice or text chat, your communication data — including voice audio streams and text messages — is routed through Photon's servers. This data is processed solely to deliver real-time communication functionality and is not used for advertising or profiling purposes.

For more information on how Photon Engine handles your data, please review their Privacy Policy at: https://www.photonengine.com/en-US/Photon/PrivacyPolicy

AGE RESTRICTIONS FOR COMMUNICATION FEATURES

The voice chat and text chat features of the Application are intended for users aged 13 and older. Users under the age of 13 are not permitted to use these features.

If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child under the age of 13 has used the chat or voice features of our Application, please contact us immediately at lta@lta-indie.com so we can take appropriate action, including the deletion of any associated data.

We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under the age of 13 through voice or chat interactions. In regions where a higher minimum age applies (such as 16 in certain EU member states), users must meet the applicable local age requirement.
